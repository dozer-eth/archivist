Privacy Policy — Archivist (Chrome Extension)
Effective date: January 21, 2026

Overview
Archivist helps users open the latest archive.is snapshot for pages on selected domains.

Data Stored Locally
Your allowlist and preferences are stored in Chrome’s storage so the extension can function.

Analytics (Opt-Out, Enabled by Default)
Archivist includes an analytics toggle in Options. When enabled, the extension sends anonymous usage analytics to Mixpanel to help improve the product. You can disable analytics in Options.

Data We Do Not Collect
We do not intentionally collect page contents, form data, or passwords.

Data Sharing
Analytics data (when enabled) is processed by Mixpanel on our behalf. We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention
Analytics data is retained according to Mixpanel’s retention settings. Local data remains until you change it or uninstall the extension.

Your Choices
Disable analytics in Options or uninstall the extension to stop data collection and remove local data.
